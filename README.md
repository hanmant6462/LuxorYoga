# Luxor Heights Art Of Living Center

Welcome to the official website repository for **Luxor Heights Art Of Living Center**. This website showcases our Yoga, Meditation, and Wellness programs, events, and provides information on how to join us.

## Features

- **Home Page**: Overview of our mission and popular programs.
- **About Us**: Information about our center and certified trainers.
- **Classes**: Details on Happiness Program, Sahaj Samadhi Meditation, and Kids Yoga.
- **Events**: Upcoming workshops and sessions.
- **Contact**: Location details, contact form, and direct WhatsApp/Social integration.

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5 & CSS3**: For structure and styling.
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: For simple interactions.
- **FontAwesome**: For icons.
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-friendly layout.
