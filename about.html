Who We Are

About Luxor Heights

We are dedicated to spreading the message of peace, health, and happiness through the ancient practices of Yoga and Meditation.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower individuals to live their lives to the fullest potential. We believe that a stress-free mind and a disease-free body are the birthright of every human being.

Affiliated with the Art of Living Foundation, we offer programs that have transformed the lives of millions around the world. Our center provides a supportive community for growth and learning.
